Contact
*******

General Contact
===============

Please use the `contact form at develmaycare.com`_ to inquire about training, commercial development or support, to
report security issues, and other questions or communications.

.. _contact form at develmaycare.com: https://develmaycare.com/contact/?product=Fixman

Issue Tracker
=============

Bugs and feature requests are logged with the `issue tracker`_. Do *not* log security issues here.

.. _issue tracker: https://github.com/develmaycare/django-fixman/issues

Support
=======

Support inquiries and other questions may be answered on StackOverflow `using the django-fixman tag`_.

.. _using the django-fixman tag: https://stackoverflow.com/questions/tagged/django-fixman
